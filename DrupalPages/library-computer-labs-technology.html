Computer Lab Area

Located in the south-west corner of the Main Floor, this area has 40 computers, a Media:Scape collaboration station, one color printer, and two black and white printers.

 

Main Floor Study Area

In the north-west corner of the Main Floor is a large open study area. There is a Media:Scape collaboration station with lounge-type seating, a color multi-function copier, one color printer, and a black and white printer.

 

Group Study Area

Next to the Computer Lab Area are group study tables separated by glass partitions. There is a black and white multi-function copier located here also.

 

Media:Scape Collaboration Stations

There are two Media:Scape Collaboration Stations located on the main floor. The Media:Scape in the Computer Lab has six VGA connections for laptops. The Media:Scape on the north end of the floor has lounge-type seating, and four HDMI connections for laptops.

 

Milne 104

Located on the Lower Floor, Milne 104 is often scheduled for classes during the day and is available for use when there are no classes using the room.  40 computers are available and one black and white printer.

 

TERC (Teacher Education Resource Center)

Located on the  Lower Floor with 7 computers. Boardmaker software is available on each computer. There is also a color and black and white printer.

 

Large Document Scanner

There is a large document scanner located on the Lower Floor of the library in the microform area. The scanner has a max scan area of 12.2" x 17.2", optical resolution up to 2400 dpi and a maximum resolution of 12,800 x 12,800 dpi. Directions are on the scanner showing you how to scan to image or PDF format.

 

VHS to DVD converter

Located just inside the CIT HelpDesk. This is a self-serve unit, just walk in and use; directions are located at the station. Please note that the conversion happens in real time so if you have a 90 minute VHS video it will take at least 90 minutes plus processing time to create your DVD.

Wireless Networks

The entire library is connected to the Geneseo Wireless Network for use with your own laptop computer.
