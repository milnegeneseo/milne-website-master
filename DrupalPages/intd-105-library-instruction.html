GOLD Workshops

Milne Library offers a research and information management related workshops as a part of Geneseo's GOLD program. See the GOLD website for a complete list of workshops and scheduled times.

Faculty and Staff Workshops

Milne library sponsors a variety of workshops for faculty and staff encouraging the informal exchange of ideas related to scholarly communication, technology, instructional design, higher education and other topics relevant to our work at SUNY Geneseo. There will often be refreshments and coffee.

If you have a topic you would like to present contact Bonnie Swoger, the Series coordinator, to get on the schedule. If there is a topic you would like to learn more about, contact Bonnie and we'll see if we can find someone to lead the workshop.
