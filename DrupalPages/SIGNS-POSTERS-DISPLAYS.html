Collection boxes

Recognized student groups or academic departments/classes may request permission to place a collection box in Milne Library using this request form.

Collection box policies:

  • We cannot accept collection boxes for money, only goods (food, household items, etc.).
  • Collection boxes will be placed on the counter near the tape and staples.
  • Boxes must include contact information (name, email and phone number) for the responsible person and clearly indicate the group or department collecting items.
  • Collection boxes will be accepted for up to two weeks at a time and must be picked up promptly at the end of the scheduled time.

E-Posters (Milne Library Lobby HDTV display)

Milne Library is happy to display electronic posters for student organizations, campus and department events that comply with our policies. Electronic posters are shown on the HDTV in the main lobby of the library in rotation along with others in a given week.

Submissions are handled on a first-come first-served basis.

Posters may be displayed for up to 2 weeks.

Milne Library E-Poster Guidelines

  • E-posters must be submitted as a single MS PowerPoint 4:3 slide in landscape orientation.
  • No other file formats will be accepted, including images (jpg, png, etc...) and PDFs.
  • If you have an image, simply place it on a PowerPoint slide, save and submit. Do not submit a 16:9 slide as the formating will change.

E-Poster Design Recommendations

  • Keep the wording simple, your e-flyer will be circulating with other submissions.
  • Be sure the text is large enough to be read from a distance.

Before you submit your poster, take one last look to be sure you have provided the following information:

  • Title of Event
  • Date(s) and Time(s)
  • Location
  • Contact information

All submissions of electronic posters for display in Milne Library are handled by Steve Dresbach. Confirmation of your submission will be made via email. Please do not assume your poster has been accepted until you receive an e-mail confirmation. Please submit your request at least three business days in advance.

Please Note: Only Geneseo faculty, staff, and recognized student organizations may post in Milne Library in accordance with the Office of Campus Scheduling & Special Events Policies and Procedures.
