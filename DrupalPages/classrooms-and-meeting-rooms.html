Classroom & Meeting Room Policies

Reservations for Milne 305 and the Lobby Table are now handled directly in Virtual EMS, the College's online booking tool. Instructions on how to make your reservation are here.

Use this form to request Room 104 and 208. All other rooms requests are handled through the campus online booking tool, Virtual EMS. To request a room for a semester-long course in Rooms 105, 109 or 213, contact the Office of the Registrar.

Reservations are generally made on a first-come first-served basis.

The following events may take priority:

  • Library instruction has priority in Room 104
  • Teaching and learning center (TLC) events have priority in Room 208

Confirmation of a reservation will be made via email within two business days.

Alternative classrooms may be suggested. Please do not assume you have the room reserved until you receive an e-mail confirmation.

For questions about room reservations or which equipment is in which room, contact Patty Hoffman (hoffman@geneseo.edu or x5523). For assistance or help with the classroom technology, contact the CIT HelpDesk (x5588).
