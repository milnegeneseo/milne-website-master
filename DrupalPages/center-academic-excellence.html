Center for Academic Excellence (CAE)

CAE Mission 

To promote educational opportunities, enhance academic achievement for all students, encourage the highest level of scholarship, and foster students' active participation in their own academic success as well as the success of their peers.  The CAE works in concert with the university's mission to develop socially responsible citizens with skills and values important to the pursuit of an enriched life and success in the world.

Writing Learning Center

Provides walk-in service for students who need writing help during the hours posted above.  ESOL writing tutors are also available. 

  • Learn more at the Writing Learning Center website.

AOP Tutoring

The Milne CAE provides space for pre-scheduled AOP tutoring.

  • If you are interested in obtaining an AOP tutor, please visit the AOP office, located in Sturges Hall, Room 120.
  • Learn more at the AOP tutoring website.

International Student & Scholar Services

The International Student & Scholars Services Office is available to assist current international students in finding academic support.

  • If you are a international student in need of assistance in finding tutorial or other support services please contact Carly O'Keefe at okeefe@geneseo.edu or call 585-245-5404 to set up an appointment.

Research Consultation

Milne Reference and Instruction Librarians provide one-on-one research consultations on a drop-in basis at the CAE, and also custom scheduled consultations with a subject specialist librarian.

  • Request an appointment online or ask a librarian.

In addition to CAE hours above, librarians are in the building for all hours the library is open and can be called upon for assistance from the Service Desk.

Testing

Testing is available in Erwin 22 by appointment with the Office of Disability Services from 8:30am - 3:30pm Monday-Friday.

  • Students with accommodated testing and faculty who have students making up exams can find information about scheduilng an exam on the Test Center's website or by emailing the Test Center at testcenter@geneseo.edu.
